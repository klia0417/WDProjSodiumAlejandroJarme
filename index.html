Welcome to the Universe of TWICE!

The global hub for every dedicated ONCE.

TWICE is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Debuting in 2015, they have achieved global success, becoming known for their bright concepts, catchy music, and incredible stage presence.
