About TWICE

TWICE was formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015 through the survival show SIXTEEN. Their debut song “Like Ooh-Ahh” marked the beginning of what would become one of the most successful girl group careers in K-pop.

The group's name, TWICE, means that they will capture people's hearts "once" through their ears and "twice" through their eyes. They are known for their bright, energetic concepts and high level of unity.

Awards / Achievements

  • First female Korean act to hold a Japanese dome tour.
  • Multiple Daesangs (Grand Prizes) at major Korean award shows.
  • First K-pop girl group to have three music videos surpass 400 million views.
  • Consistently rank among the top-selling K-pop girl groups of all time.

Milestone Timeline

  • **2015 Oct:** Debut with *The Story Begins* and "Like Ooh-Ahh".
  • **2016 Apr:** Achieved first major success with *Page Two* and "Cheer Up".
  • **2017 Jun:** Debut in Japan with *#TWICE*.
  • **2019 Sep:** Released *Feel Special*, showing a more mature concept.
  • **2023 Mar:** Started the record-breaking *READY TO BE* World Tour.
