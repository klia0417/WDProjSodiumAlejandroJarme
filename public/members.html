Meet the Members

Nayeon

Lead Vocalist, Lead Dancer, Center, Face of the Group

Full Name: Im Na-yeon (임나연)

Birthday: September 22, 1995

Nationality: South Korean

Zodiac Sign: Virgo

Height: 163 cm

MBTI: ISTP

Hobbies: Listening to music, fashion styling, exercising

Facts about Nayeon:

  • Born in Myeongil-dong, Gangdong-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
  • Has a younger sister, Seoyeon (born 1998).
  • Cousin Ji Yunseo is a member of the project boy group EVNNE.
  • Oldest member of TWICE.
  • Chosen for JYP’s girl group 6mix, which disbanded before debut.
  • First SIXTEEN member to be announced and first confirmed in TWICE.
  • Favorite color: Purple, Beige, Sky Blue, and Khaki.
  • Favorite number: 9, because TWICE has 9 members.
  • Left leg is weak due to a childhood car accident.
  • Close friends with BLACKPINK members Jisoo and Jennie.
  • Debuted as a solo artist on June 24, 2022, with mini-album IM NAYEON.

Jeongyeon

Lead Vocalist

Full Name: Yoo Jeong-yeon (유정연)

Birthday: November 1, 1996

Nationality: South Korean

Zodiac Sign: Scorpio

Height: 169 cm

MBTI: ESFJ

Hobbies: Watching movies, sports, photography

Facts about Jeongyeon:

  • Born in Jeonja-dong, Jangan, Suwon, South Korea.
  • Birth name: Yoo Kyungwan. She changed it in 3rd grade because she was teased for having a boyish name.
  • Father, Yoo Changjoon, is a popular Korean traditional chef.
  • Has two older sisters: Gong Seungyeon (actress) and Seoyeon (office worker).
  • Distant relative of KARD‘s Somin.
  • Leader of TWICE was decided by anonymous voting; she took 2nd place.
  • Favorite color: Black and Purple.
  • Favorite number: 0, because she doesn’t actually have one.
  • Says the happiest part of her day is taking off her contact lenses.
  • Ambidextrous (TWICE Showtime).
  • Used to be in charge of cleaning the dorm; enjoys cleaning.

Momo

Main Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Sub-Rapper

Full Name: Hirai Momo (平井 もも)

Birthday: November 9, 1996

Nationality: Japanese

Zodiac Sign: Scorpio

Height: 167 cm

MBTI: INTP

Hobbies: Dancing, cooking, fitness

Facts about Momo:

  • Born in Kyōtanabe, Kyoto, Japan.
  • Has an older sister, Hana, born in 1994.
  • Scouted by JYP Entertainment after they saw a dance video of her and her sister.
  • Passed the audition on April 13, 2012; her sister did not make it.
  • Eliminated in episode 6 of SIXTEEN, but J.Y. Park added her to TWICE for her dancing skills.
  • Name means “peach” in Japanese.
  • Favorite number is 64, from her father’s football shirt number.
  • She and GOT7‘s BamBam both have moms who are big fans of Rain.
  • Her family has three female dogs: Petco, Pudding, and Lucky.
